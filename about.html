FAQs

Are there ID or minimum age requirements to enter the event?
This event is for theatre artists ages 13-18. It is specifically formulated for this age range, so while we are willing to make accommodations, please keep this in mind.

How can I contact the organizer with any questions?
Email us at information@academymasterclasses.com or submit a message through our contact page.

What should I bring to the event?
You will be emailed with specific requirements depending on the classes you decide to take however, it is advised that everyone should bring a small bag, a refillable water bottle, and comfortable shoes.
If you are planning on taking a coaching class, please be prepared with the materials necessary, such as a cut of a monologue or a song with sheet music.
If you do not purchase a lunch ticket and you are staying for a full day of classes, please pack a lunch.

What's the refund policy?
We will provide refunds for any cancellations until November 17th.
